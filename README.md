# FitTrack Pro - Modern Fitness Dashboard

FitTrack Pro is a feature-rich, responsive web application designed for personal health and fitness management. It offers a sleek user interface with real-time progress tracking, habit management, and a dual-theme experience.

* **Interactive Workout Journal**: Log your exercises, sets, and reps with automatic category tagging.
* **Dynamic Daily Goal**: A progress bar that automatically calculates and displays how close you are to your daily fitness target (Default Goal: 5 workouts).
* **BMI Calculator**: Integrated tool to instantly calculate Body Mass Index with visual health status indicators.
* **Habit & Water Tracker**: Monitor your daily hydration (target 8 glasses) and essential wellness habits like sleep and nutrition.
* **Theme Toggle**: Seamlessly switch between Dark and Light modes depending on your preference.
* **Data Persistence**: All your logs and habits are saved in `localStorage`, so your data remains even after closing the browser.
